Standard for Socialvoid network names

A username must be at least 3 characters long
A username cannot be larger than 20 characters (?)

Can contain underscores "_"
Can contain "A-Z" & "a-z" characters
Can contain "0-9" digits as characters

Cannot contain only numbers
Cannot begin with "_"
Cannot end with "_"
Cannot contain any other special characters other than the mentioned above, "A-Z, a-z & _" (Not including -)

Regex, created by @Justasic

^(?!_)[A-Za-z0-9_]{3,20}(?<!_)$
